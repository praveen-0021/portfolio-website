About Me

The Story
So Far

I am Praveen, a second-year Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) student at IDSG Government College, Chikkamagaluru. My academic journey has ignited a deep passion for technology, programming, and digital problem-solving.

"Technology is best when it brings people together — and I aim to be the bridge."

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is a rigorous three-year undergraduate degree that lays a comprehensive foundation in computer science, software engineering, and information technology. The curriculum spans core programming disciplines, relational database systems, software development methodologies, networking fundamentals, and modern web technologies — equipping students with both theoretical depth and hands-on technical capability. BCA graduates are primed for careers across software development, IT consulting, systems analysis, and digital product design, making it one of the most versatile and future-forward undergraduate programmes in the technology space.

Outside academics, I channel my curiosity into coding projects, reading, and music — always seeking to grow both technically and personally.
